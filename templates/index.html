The Logic Behind AgroAssist

How I transformed agricultural data into actionable insights

Profitability-Based Crop Recommendation

Profitability Score = (Yield √ó Price - Cost) / Cost

I developed an innovative crop recommendation system based on profitability rather than just yield. The algorithm works through:

  • Machine learning models to predict crop yields based on soil and climate data
  • SARIMA (Seasonal ARIMA) models to forecast future crop prices with seasonal adjustments
  • Cost of production estimation based on regional input costs
  • Profitability score calculation to rank crop choices

This approach ensures farmers not only grow what thrives in their conditions, but what will maximize their return on investment.

CNN-Based Pest Prediction

I implemented a sophisticated pest detection system using Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) that:

  • Analyzes images of plant leaves to identify early signs of pest infestation
  • Classifies multiple pest species with over 92% accuracy
  • Provides treatment recommendations specific to the identified pest
  • Works offline after initial model download for use in remote areas

The CNN model was trained on a dataset of 20,000+ images covering common agricultural pests in various lighting conditions and growth stages, ensuring robust detection in real-world conditions.
